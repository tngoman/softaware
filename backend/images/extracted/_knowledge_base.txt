## EFT Payment Notice
Payments made via EFT without a reference will be credited to Silulumanzi's unallocated fund account, which could result in the customer's water account not being settled. It is the customer's responsibility to provide proof of payment to have funds diverted back. EFT payments should be made 3–4 days before the 7th of each month due to bank delays.

## Debit Order Setup
Benefits: automatic payments, better payment tracking, no missed payments, payments on the correct day each month.
How to set up:
1. Visit Silulumanzi customer service offices in Mbombela City, KaNyamazane, or Matsulu.
2. Speak to a Credit Controller Officer and sign a debit order instruction form with your bank details.
3. Silulumanzi will approach your bank for permission to collect.
Note: Banks charge a fee for each debit order (fees vary). Tip: Add 10% to your monthly average usage billing amount for fluctuations.

## Payment Deadline Notice
All Silulumanzi accounts must be paid before the 7th of each month. Unpaid bills may result in further interest charges, penalties, and serious credit control steps.

## How to Avoid Estimated Billing
Take a picture of your meter reading (showing meter numbers) and email it to enquiries@silulumanzi.com with your account number.
